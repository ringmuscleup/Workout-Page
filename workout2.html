Calistenic Goals 2020

Warm-Up

  • Blood flow: 10-20 burpees, 60s crawling
  • Mobility: 15x Wrist Circles, Shoulder Circles, Bodyweight Squats, and any other bodypart that needs to be wermed up, 60s of support hold work (parallel bars or rings or chairs/counters), 5x Skin the Cat/German Hangs.
  • Positional Drills: 30-60s of Plank, Both Side planks, Reverse Plank, Hollow, and Arch Holds.

Skill Work

5-10 Minutes of Handstand Work, performed against the wall/ Tricking and Tumbling.

Strength Work

  • Push
  • Pull
  • Both

Push

  • Push: 4x5-15 Handstand Push progression 3-4 minutes of rest
  • Pull: easy to mid difficulty (archer, l-sit) 4x5-15 3-4 minutes of rest
  • Dip variations: 4x5-15 with 3-4 minutes of rest
  • Pistol Squats: 3x5-15 with 3 minutes of rest
  • L-sit/Manna Progression 60seconds total in as many sets needed

Pull

  • Pull: One arm pull up progression 4x5-15. 3-4 minutes of rest.
  • Push: light-intermediate Planche work 4x5-15
  • Front Lever work 4x5-15
  • intermediate ring muscle up work
  • L-sit/Manna Progression 60seconds total in as many sets needed, rings optional

Both

  • Push: Intermediate to heavy Planche work 4x5-15
  • Pull:light-intermediate one armed pull up work 4x5-15
  • Push: intermediate inverted pushing work 4x5-15
  • Pull: intermediate front lever work
  • L-sit/Manna Progression

Prehabilitation, Isolation Work, Flexibility Work, and Cool Down.

  • 3x8 fingertip pushups
  • 3-5x30s Splits Holds/Pike Hold
  • 3-5x30s German Hangs
  • 3-5x20s Back Bridges
  • 1 minute of deep breathing

Mesocycle Info

  • A preparatory phase tends to be a slow increase in volume and intensity coming up from an off week. It was basically a "ramp in" for a serious workout directly after a deadload week.
  • A hypertrophy phase tends to range in about 60-85% 1RM (about 5-12 repetitions 1-3 minute rest times).
  • A strength phase tends to range around about 80-100% 1RM (about 1-8 reps 3 minute rest times).
  • A power phase tends to utilize lighter weights around 40-60% 1RM (about 3-6 repetitions) and focuses on pure acceleration.
